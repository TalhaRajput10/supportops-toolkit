# SQL Support Scenarios

## Scenario 1: Customer Cannot Log In

Problem:
A customer reports they cannot log in to their account.

Investigation:
- Verify that the account exists.
- Check whether the account is active.
- Confirm the email address is correct.
- Look for any account lock or suspension status.

Outcome:
Determine whether the issue is related to the customer's account or another system.

---

## Scenario 2: Customer Cannot Find Their Order

Problem:
The customer says their recent order is missing.

Investigation:
- Search for the order using the customer's email or order ID.
- Verify the order status.
- Check whether payment was successful.

Outcome:
Determine whether the order exists and identify its current status.

---

## Scenario 3: Customer Reports Duplicate Charges

Problem:
The customer believes they were charged twice.

Investigation:
- Review payment records.
- Compare transaction IDs.
- Check payment timestamps.

Outcome:
Determine whether duplicate transactions occurred or if one payment is pending.

---

## Key Takeaway

SQL helps Technical Support Engineers investigate issues by retrieving accurate information from the database before taking further action.
