# Case Study: Customer Unable to Log In

## Overview

This case study demonstrates a structured approach to troubleshooting a login issue reported by a customer.

---

## Customer Report

The customer contacted support stating:

> "I cannot log in to my account. It keeps saying my credentials are invalid."

---

## Investigation

### Step 1: Gather Information

Requested:

- Registered email address
- Screenshot of the error
- Browser and device
- Time the issue occurred

---

### Step 2: Verify Account

Checked:

- Account exists
- Account status is Active
- Email address matches customer records

---

### Step 3: Authentication Checks

Verified:

- Password reset link sent successfully
- Customer reset password
- Login issue persisted

---

### Step 4: Browser Testing

Customer tested:

- Incognito Mode
- Different Browser

Issue still occurred.

---

### Step 5: Service Status

Checked:

- Authentication Service

No outage detected.

---

## Root Cause

The customer's account had Multi-Factor Authentication enabled, but the authenticator application was no longer linked to their device.

---

## Resolution

- Verified customer identity.
- Reset MFA.
- Customer completed MFA setup.
- Login successful.

---

## Lessons Learned

- Always verify account status before escalating.
- Gather complete information before troubleshooting.
- Test client-side issues first.
- Escalate only after eliminating common causes.
