# Case Study: Customer Unable to Log In

## Issue

The customer reported they were unable to log in to their account and received an "Invalid Credentials" error.

---

## Investigation

- Verified the customer's account existed.
- Confirmed the account was active.
- Asked the customer to reset their password.
- Tested login using an Incognito browser.
- Checked for any ongoing authentication service outages.

---

## Root Cause

The customer's password had expired and required a reset.

---

## Resolution

- Sent a password reset link.
- Customer created a new password.
- Verified successful login.

---

## Lessons Learned

- Always verify account status first.
- Gather all relevant information before troubleshooting.
- Rule out browser or network issues before escalating.
